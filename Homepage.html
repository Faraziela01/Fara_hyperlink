An idol


Jeon Jungkook was born in September 1,1997 better known mononymously as Jungkoos is a South Korea singer and songwritter.He is the youngest member and vocalist of the South Korea boyband,BTS.



His family consists of his parents and an elder brother.He attended Baekyang Elementary and middle school in Busan.When he become a trainee at Bighit Entertaiment,he transferred to Singu Middle school in Seoul.Jungkook initially had dreams of becoming a badminton player when he was young,but after seeing G-Dragon perform ''heartbreaker'' on television,it influenced him to become a singer.



On june 4,2020,Jungkook released the song ''still with you'' for free on platforms such as Soundcloud as a part of BTS yearly debut celebrations.This song has become one of the songs that really helps Jungkook fans including me to release their stress and sadness.
